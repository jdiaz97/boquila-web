    About Us

    We are a non-profit foundation established in 2023 with the aim of creating a stronger connection between technology and nature.

    We use science and technology as tools to promote environmental sustainability

    We are based on the following pillars:

    • Scientific research
    • Creation of cutting-edge technologies
    • Real-world applications to promote sustainability

    Chile has a long list of environmental issues to address, and we want to help solve them. We believe that multidisciplinary effort and community involvement can lead us to the sustainable development of the country and the region.

    Fields of work

    We are making progress in

    Artificial intelligence

    We develop neural network models for multiple needs. We work with the latest technologies and best architectures.

    IoT

    We work with embedded systems and apply them to environmental and natural resources issues.

    Robotics

    We are designing and developing software for small robotic systems with various applications to promote sustainability in the agricultural sector.

    Native flora propagation

    We sustainably collect seeds of native flora in order to propagate and promote them among local communities.

    Ecological restoration

    We design and implement restorations at various scales, always with the focus of recovering a degraded environment to its natural state.

    Sustainable landscaping

    We aim to create human environments that are sustainable in terms of natural resources and the climate reality.

    Projects

    This is a list of some of the projects we are working on

    Fauna Classifiers

    Neural Networks - Conservation

    We use PyTorch and a large amount of labeled data to build a high-performance neural network capable of classifying animals in videos and images.

    Low-Cost Irrigation System for Nurseries

    IoT - Native Flora Propagation

    We design a low-cost irrigation system using embedded systems and a mobile application for control. Designed for forest nurseries.

    Automated terrestrial bioacustics

    Neural networks - Ecology

    We develop models and algorithms capable of studying the biodviersity of a place through audio and generating reports in an automated manner.

    Automated marine bioacustics

    Neural networks - Ecology

    We develop models and algorithms capable of analyzing data generated through hydrophones to obtain valuable information regarding the abundance and distribution of marine species.

    AI Wildfire Detector

    Neural Networks - IoT

    We develop a high-performance artificial intelligence to be mounted on low-cost modular systems. Its goal is to observe and provide early warnings for potential forest fires.
